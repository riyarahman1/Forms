Course in

Cinematography

This course enables students to acquire technical knowledge and skills of cinematography through structured workshops and joint projects. Cinematography students get to work with masters on filmsets and are exposed to get networking opportunities. The goal of becoming a good cinematographer can be achieved through systematic project studies, self-direction and workshops in special projects. Once the course gets completed, the students will have expertise in various camera systems, lighting patterns and handling of visual tools and equipment’s. The students get a chance to experience the work flow in on going movie projects.

Course in

Editing

As part of the curriculum, theory and practical exercises are carried out with importance in both creative and technical part of the post- production process of film making. The course gives insight into the work flow of a film editor who works with the raw footage, selects shots and combines them into sequences which create a finished motion picture.Course offers practical knowledge in film editing by giving released movie rough footages and permitting students to experience on going movie project edit process flow. Training on editing software like Adobe After Effects, FCP and Adobe Premiere Pro are provided with practical exposure.
