WHY CHOOSE US?



REACH YOUR DREAM DESTINATION

Elements of Cinema develops professional skills among the students in the core areas of Filmmaking - Direction, Acting, Screenwriting, Film Editing, Dubbing, Cinematography, and Still Photography- by offering professional and practical-oriented study programs.

Sreeraj M, an enterprising business personality and a visionary movie lover, ventured into starting a film academy to mold and create talented technicians and artists for the bright future of Cinema. While making the dream come true for many film aspirants to enter the film world, EOC is committed to providing quality training and exposure to filmmaking through experienced faculty members and known personalities from the film industry. We welcome you to Elements of Cinema, “EOC”, and sincerely hope that you find us the right platform to begin your professional education in the world of Cinema.
